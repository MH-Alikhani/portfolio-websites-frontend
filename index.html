FourFold Consulting helps businesses grow and transform.

We believe sustainable change happens when there is alignment between the commercial and human aspects of a business. This thoughtful integration of finance and strategy with leadership and culture is what we know and do.

Our Service

By focusing on profits and people, we help leaders
understand how to deploy their biggest sources of capital. We do this across four key areas.

Purpose & Strategy

We support organisations to discover, refine and realise their purpose and strategy. We help think through the financial, competitive, and societal impacts of choices with the aim of running more profitable and responsible businesses.

    We will:

  • Uncover or discover purpose
  • Guide how to govern, lead and live purpose
  • Define and review strategic plans, vision and values

Value & Growth

We work with organisations to size, prioritise and realise opportunities so they can grow, transform and build strategic agility. We also assess organisational capabilities and help evolve them for successful execution.

    We will:

  • Identify revenue and cost opportunities
  • Review acquisition and divestment options
  • Define and sequence transformation and capability agendas

Leadership & Culture

We work with CEOs and leaders to equip them and their teams to get ahead, get things done and get along. We help understand existing leadership routines and cultural habits and how these need to shift to achieve results.

    We will:

  • Clarify the role, priorities and behaviours for an effective top team
  • Design and facilitate leadership programs and executive coaching
  • Identify areas of misalignment and outline a plan for change

Models & Systems

We help organisations explore the effectiveness of current operating models and structures to achieve goals. We define how best to organise work and set reporting and metrics to deliver results with impact and engagement.

    We will:

  • Align organisational principles, structures and incentives with strategy
  • Improve business metrics, controls and management reporting
  • Translate strategic direction to technology and customer implications

Our Leaders

With experience spanning multiple industries, business types and functions, we know what it takes to achieve results in large established companies, both public and private, and to build from the ground up in smaller businesses.

Anita Fleming

Co-Founder and Director

Anita works with senior leaders across industries to answer business questions about direction, capability, culture and change. From her time at ANZ, Kinrise, Rio Tinto and Hay Group, she brings differentiated experience in linking purpose with strategy, aligning teams and coaching leaders to translate business ambition into reality.

Prior to FourFold Consulting, Anita was General Manager People and Culture at Kinrise. Working closely with the CEO, she helped to integrate several independent food manufacturing businesses to form Kinrise, including establishing the executive team, defining the business strategy, operating model and transformation priorities, flexing between growth and cost agendas, and coordinating the COVID-19 business-wide response.

Kynwynn Strong

Co-Founder and Director

Kynwynn works with businesses to define strategy and improve profitability. She has extensive experience in financial analysis, valuation, and competitive positioning. As evidenced in her roles at Goldman Sachs, Racing.com and a digital start up, Kynwynn works hand in hand with CEOs and CHROs to drive long term success.

Prior to FourFold Consulting, Kynwynn was the General Manager of Finance and Strategy at Racing.com. In this role she helped transform a disaggregated business into an integrated, cross-functional entity. At the same time, she led the internalisation and restructuring of the finance function to enable greater oversight and control.
